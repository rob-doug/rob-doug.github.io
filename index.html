Change a Child's Life Today

Youth around the Seattle area need a positive mentor in their lives. Mentor Central connects adults interested in becoming mentors with organizations in need of mentors.

Why Mentor?

Mentoring is super fun! You'll learn about yourself, and give back to your community. Help end the cycle of poverty by being a positive influence for a youth in need. One hour can help change a life.

Mentoring Isn't All Serious

It's rewarding, it's great experience, and it can change lives!
